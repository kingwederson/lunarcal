Welcome to Lunarcal, a lunisolar calendar converter observing ancient Hebrew way of time counting.

Please do not mistake this lunisolar calendar with Hebrew Calendar (a.k.a Jewish Calendar). Read explanations below to understand the diference. Futhermore if you want convert some dates go on using calculators below.

What is a lunisolar calendar?

Lunisolar calendars are calendars that align the cycles of Sun (years) and Moon (months) hitting a syncrony. In contrast with Gregorian Calendar, Indian Calendar or Julian Calendar, that are somewhat supposed to be ajusted with tropical year which month are a fraction of it, a lunissolar calendars includes the cycles of Moon period to mensure the months.
